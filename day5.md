Comparison:

Without Context:

Generic daily schedule.

Starts from scratch assumptions.

"Learn web dev" broadly.

Mixed format.

Beginner-safe pacing.

With Context:

Respects a student's exam periods or a freelancer's client deadlines.

Skips what you know, spends more time on gaps.

"Get a junior job" vs. "build a side project" leads to very different week 4 content.

All videos, or all projects, or reading-heavy — structure changes entirely.

Intermediate learners skip basics, go deeper faster.
