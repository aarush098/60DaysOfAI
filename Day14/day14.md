## Generated Report

Overall Risk Score: 87/100 (Very High Risk)

Top Red Flags

"Junior" role demanding 5+ years experience — severity 9 (fundamental contradiction; likely underpaying for senior-level asks)

Impossibly broad tech stack (3 frontend frameworks, 4 backend languages, 3 cloud platforms, Docker/K8s, AI/ML) — severity 9 (no one person masters this; sign of no real scoping)

"24/7 support when needed" — severity 9 (classic burnout/on-call-without-boundaries language)

Fake remote job — "Remote (relocation to Bengaluru may be required after probation)" — severity 8 (bait-and-switch on a core benefit)

"We are like a family" + "must thrive under pressure" + "work hard, play hard" — severity 7 (hustle-culture euphemisms correlating with poor boundaries)

No salary range disclosed — severity 6 (transparency red flag, wastes candidate time)

Junior expected to lead client meetings, mentor others, and own architecture — severity 8 (role scope of 3-4 senior jobs bundled into one junior title)

Rapid hiring phase at an 8-month-old, 12-person, unfunded startup — severity 7 (possible high turnover backfill or desperation hiring)

Employee reviews already citing long hours this early — severity 8 (toxic culture forming before company has even stabilized)

Positive Signals

Explicit mention of AI/ML exposure could mean interesting/modern tech work

Small team size could mean high visibility and fast learning curve (if culture were healthy)

Transparent that reviews mention long hours — at least the info surfaced (via Glassdoor-type sources), even if company itself didn't disclose it

5 Smart Interview Questions

"This is listed as a junior role but requires 5+ years of experience across a very wide stack — can you clarify what the actual expected seniority and core tech focus are for day one?"
(Validates: title/requirement mismatch, real scope)

"Can you walk me through what a typical week looks like, including how often the 24/7 support requirement is actually invoked, and how on-call is compensated or rotated?"
(Validates: burnout risk, real workload)

"The listing says remote with possible relocation to Bengaluru after probation — under what circumstances would that be triggered, and is it optional or mandatory?"
(Validates: remote authenticity, hidden office requirement)

"What's the current funding status and runway of the company, and how has the team's size changed over the last 6 months?"
(Validates: company stability, turnover/layoff risk)

"Given this role touches architecture, client meetings, mentoring, and cloud infra — how is success measured, and who else on the team shares these responsibilities?"
(Validates: role clarity, whether one person is expected to do 3-4 jobs
